    About BookBuddy

    BookBuddy helps you discover new books and keep track of your reading journey. Your log is saved locally for your convenience.

    FAQ

    How are recommendations generated?

    We use a curated set of books for each genre. In a real app, this could be powered by a books API.

    Is my reading log stored online?

    No, your log is stored only in your browser.

    Can I suggest a feature?

    Yes! Please open an issue or contribute on GitHub.
