Indian Historical Leaders

Great leaders who shaped the history of India

Mahatma Gandhi

Leader of India's non-violent freedom movement and Father of the Nation.

Jawaharlal Nehru

First Prime Minister of India and a key leader of independence.

Sardar Patel

Known as the Iron Man of India, united princely states.

Subhas Chandra Bose

Leader of the Indian National Army and freedom fighter.

Bhagat Singh

Revolutionary who sacrificed his life for India's freedom.

Lal Bahadur Shastri

Second Prime Minister of India, known for simplicity.

Rani Lakshmibai

Queen of Jhansi and brave warrior of 1857 revolt.

Bal Gangadhar Tilak

Freedom fighter who popularized the slogan "Swaraj is my birthright".
