About Dark Matter

Dark matter is invisible matter that doesn't emit light but affects galaxy rotation. The flat rotation curve suggests dark matter halos around galaxies.

  • Keplerian: Velocity decreases with distance (no dark matter)
  • Observed: Velocity remains constant (with dark matter)
